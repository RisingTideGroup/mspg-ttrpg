How the Race Works

Three major segments. Five tiles each. Fifteen tiles total. One die per turn — all tables roll simultaneously. Phase 1 attribute investment is your effective modifier against each tile's DC. Beat the DC to advance. Beat it by 5+ to surge. Miss it to stay. Natural 1 has special consequences.

The Single Roller
One player per table rolls each turn. Any player — can change every turn, no restriction. Roll one d20, add the effective attribute modifier for the current tile type, compare to tile DC. Group rolls are Phase 1 only. One die. One roller. One result.
Initiative — Turn Order
Round 1 — Group InitiativeAll players roll d20. GM sums and divides by player count (round down). Highest average goes first. Ties: re-roll between tied tables only.
Round 2+ — Reverse GridLast place goes first. First place goes last. Same-tile position ties: MC picks the most interesting moment. Trailing table goes first AND gets Boost.

⚙ PHASE 3 — ROUND STRUCTURE & MECHANICS Round Steps · Battles · Boost · Sponsors · Winning ⚙
Round Structure — 6 Steps Per Round
1
Open Round MC opens the round. Resolve any battle markers from the prior round before anything else.
2
Boost Check Last-place table 3+ tiles behind next car ahead? Boost fires. Boost recipient rolls foam d6, selects result aloud. Natural 1 crews always top priority regardless of gap.
3
Sponsor Window All sponsor uses declared before any dice are touched. Positive or negative. Out loud. MC acknowledges each. Uses expended on declaration regardless of outcome.
4
All Tables Roll All 8 tables roll simultaneously. GMs calculate effective modifier for current tile type. All tables hold results — no movement yet.
5
MC Calls Results Last-to-first (reverse grid). GM confirms modified total. MC resolves and narrates. 30 sec per table max. Move tokens after each call.
6
Open Next Round MC updates tracker. Re-orders by new grid position. Battle markers appear for shared tiles. Open Round N+1.
Battles
TriggerTwo or more tables on the same tile at end of round. Automatic.
No-Battle ZoneTiles 1-1 through 1-4. First legal battle: tile 1-5 (Exit Hairpin).
The RollEach table's roller: 1d20 + effective modifier for tile type. All roll simultaneously. Highest modified result wins. Ties re-roll once.
WinnerAdvance 1 additional tile immediately. MC calls the overtake to the room.
LoserStay on tile. Falling behind is the penalty.
3+ TablesAll roll simultaneously. Highest wins the advance. All others stay.
Trailing Table Boost — 5 Options

Fires when last-place is 3+ tiles behind next car. One table max. Resolved before Sponsor Window. Natural 1 crews always jump to top of priority.

Slipstream Choose one attribute. Effective modifier for that attribute is doubled this turn. Declare before rolling.
Crowd Surge +3 added to d20 result after the roll. MC announces before dice are picked up.
Arcane Repair Treat tile as neutral — no modifier applies (positive or negative). Raw dice only. Useful on brutal off-attribute tiles.
Wild Fuel Cell No roll required. Move 2 tiles immediately. Takes the place of normal roll. MC announces dramatically.
Ghost Draft Use the highest d20 already rolled this round (any table) as your result, then add your own effective modifier. The leader's momentum becomes yours.
Boost Rules — Pacing Tool
MC discretion — do not consider before Round 16. All tile DCs drop by 2 for the remainder of the current segment. Announce publicly before the round begins. Fires in roughly 1-in-4 races. Can be lifted at next segment boundary — MC's call.

Winning the Race
🏁 Race ends when first table clears tile 3-5 (The Finish Straight) The round completes — every remaining table rolls their turn. Final placement by grid position at end of the round. Photo Finish: two or more tables clear in the same round → raw d20 roll-off per tied table, no modifiers, simultaneous. Ties re-roll until broken.
