The Three Attributes
VELOCITYRaw top-end pace. Affects Velocity tile rolls.
HANDLINGCornering & precision. Affects Handling tile rolls.

All three attributes start at −2. Investment during Phase 1 is the only way to climb. It is mathematically impossible to max all three within 100g.

The Group Roll
All Phase 1 challenges use the same mechanic. Every player rolls a d20 simultaneously. GM sums all results and divides by player count (round down). If the average beats the DC, the challenge succeeds.
Example: 6 players roll 14, 8, 19, 11, 6, 16. Sum = 74 ÷ 6 = 12.3 → round down = 12. Beats DC 12. Fails DC 13.
DC 12 (Upgrade): ~60–65% success. Groups usually make this. DC 14 (Champ Spec): ~35–40% success. Should feel genuinely risky. Natural 20s from any player: announce them individually before calculating the average.
Gold Budget
100g per crew. Unspent gold is lost when Phase 1 ends — no bank.
Focused build (two strong attributes): ~55–65g. Balanced build (three at 0 or better): ~60–70g.
Encourage crews to complete the Vendor Route Planner before spending their first gold.
