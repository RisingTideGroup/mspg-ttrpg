⚙ GM TABLE DM BRIEF Phase-by-Phase Responsibilities · Phase 1 & 2 ⚙
PHASE 1 — Parts Buying ~60 min Involvement: Maximum

You are the busiest person in the room during Phase 1. Play NPC vendors, take gold, run challenge rolls, notate final VEL / HAN / RES modifiers on the build sheet, and get the crew ready for Phase 2.

Setup
  • Two sensory details per district from the Sensory Atlas, then stop. Let players ask questions.
  • 100g budget. Unspent gold is lost — no bank. All attributes start at −2. Impossible to max all three.
  • Vendor Route Planner — 5-stop grid on the player card. Encourage crews to complete it before spending their first gold.

End of Phase 1 Checklist
Total gold spent ≤ 100g. Remind table of any remaining gold — it will be lost.
All three modifiers (VEL / HAN / RES) confirmed and circled on every character sheet and the build sheet.
Crew has team name, construct drawn on the whiteboard, and sponsor pitch prepared.
Signal the MC when done. MC may start Phase 2 at 65+ min without waiting for the full room.
PHASE 2 — Sponsor Pavilion ~20 min Involvement: Low

The MC runs the room. The prep you drove at end of Phase 1 now plays out. One designated crew rep delivers an RP pitch; your job was to help them prepare it. Once your table has presented, your involvement is minimal — use this window to review Phase 3 mechanics.

Presentation Logistics
  • Confirm crew name, construct name, and 30–60 sec pitch are ready before the table is called.
  • Record sponsor tier / uses / modifier / patron name on every player card immediately after MC assigns.
  • Natural 1 result: be ready to be named in-character as their backer. Two uses of ±1 is still real value. Play warmly, not apologetically.
Initiative Roll — End of Phase 2
All players roll d20 simultaneously on MC's count. Sum ÷ player count (round down). Call score to MC. Ties: re-roll between tied tables only. This sets Round 1 turn order. From Round 2 onward: reverse grid always — last place rolls first, first place rolls last.

Core Loop — Each Round
1
Sponsor window — MC opens before any dice. Help your table decide: boost own roll or penalise another team. Remind uncertain players of remaining uses. Uses expended on declaration regardless of outcome.
2
Choose your roller — MC calls for it. Encourage players who match the current tile type's attribute or haven't rolled recently. Remind roller they can declare a personal boost now if it applies.
3
All tables roll simultaneously on MC's prompt. Hold your result — do not announce until the MC calls your table.
4
When called — report d20 + effective modifier + tile type. Cross off any sponsor use or character boost and confirm with MC. Narrate to your table (30 sec max) — MC assists if needed.
5
MC resolves battles and Boost. Have your roller ready and know your next tile type.
Master loop: MC opens sponsor window → MC calls for roller (you encourage right player) → all tables roll → MC calls last-to-first → you report + tally uses → narrate → MC moves token → MC resolves battles / Boost.

Narration
  • Two sensory details per tile from the Atlas, delivered crisply. Stop. Answer player questions specifically.
  • Blazing Run: back the MC's room announcement with a strong table moment.
  • Natural 1, bottom 50%: Boost next round (★). Narrate a construct hiccup.
  • Natural 1, top 50%: Lose next turn (SKIP). Same narration — different stakes.
  • Aether Bridge (3-3, DC 15 only): slow down. Describe the 1.7-second engine silence first.
  • After the finish: one breath of quiet. Ask each player what their character does in the 30 seconds after crossing the line.

Other Mechanics — MC Handles, You Support
  • Battles: 2+ tables on same tile at end of round. MC calls it. Each table's roller: d20 + modifier. Highest wins, advances 1 extra tile. No battles on tiles 1-1 through 1-4.
  • SKIP tokens: Natural 1, top-50% field. Lose next turn. Confirm with MC, clear token after skipped round.
  • Boost Rules (MC discretion, Round 16 minimum): all tile DCs drop by 2. MC announces. No GM action needed.
