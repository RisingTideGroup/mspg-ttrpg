⚔ Battle
Roll initiative first · d20 + PERCEPTION mod
Movement — up to your speed per turn
Move
Travel up to your speed (ft). Can split around action.
Disengage
Move without triggering opportunity attacks. Uses action.
Dash
Double your movement this turn. Uses action.
Action — one per turn
Attack
Roll d20 + VEL mod to hit. Roll weapon damage on hit.
Shove
Contest: d20 + VEL vs target d20 + RES. Win = push 5ft.
Grapple
Contest: d20 + VEL vs target d20 + HAN. Win = restrain.
Help
Give ally advantage on their next attack or ability roll.
Hide
d20 + HAN vs enemy PERCEPTION. Success = hidden.
Use Item
Activate a vendor part, field kit, or tool from inventory.
Bonus Action — one per turn, optional
Quick Taunt
d20 + HAG vs target PERCEPTION. Disadvantage on their next roll.
Scan
Ask the GM one yes/no question about this encounter.
Assist Crew
Add +1 to any adjacent crewmate's roll this round.
