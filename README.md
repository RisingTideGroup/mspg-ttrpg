# mspg-ttrpg
MSPGeekCon TTRPG Planning and Organization

**A community TTRPG event produced by [Rising Tide Group](https://risingtidegroup.net) for [MSPGeekCon](https://mspgeekcon.com) — now in its fourth year.**

---

## What Is This?

Each year at MSPGeekCon, Rising Tide Group produces a unique tabletop role-playing game one-shot built around the **Gootsocra** — an original race of bird-people created as a love letter to [Goots](https://mspgeekcon.com/about-mspgeekcon/goots/), MSPGeek's beloved goose mascot, and the Aarakocra of D&D lore.

The Gootsocra are smart, scrappy, and take no $#!%. They navigate the world as a group. Sound familiar?

Every year's adventure is a standalone story: a new theme, new mechanics, new chaos. No prior TTRPG experience required. Beginners, veterans, and everyone in between share the same table — and that's exactly the point.

> *"We came to the table mostly strangers and left as friends."*
> — El Copeland, MSPGeekCon 2023
